How do I schedule an appointment for my pet?

To schedule an appointment for your pet, follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your client portal account
  2. Click on the "Appointments" tab in the navigation menu
  3. Click the "Book New Appointment" button
  4. Select your pet, the service needed, and your preferred date and time
  5. Review and confirm your appointment details

You'll receive a confirmation email once your appointment is scheduled, and you can always view or reschedule it from the Appointments section.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
  • GCash
  • PayMaya
  • Bank Transfer
  • Cash (for in-clinic payments only)

You can manage your payment methods in the Payments section of your client portal.

How do I update my pet's information?

To update your pet's information:

  1. Navigate to the "My Pets" section in the client portal
  2. Find your pet's profile card and click on "View Details" or "Edit"
  3. Update the necessary information (weight, diet, medications, etc.)
  4. Click "Save Changes" to update your pet's profile

Keeping your pet's information up-to-date helps our veterinarians provide the best care possible.

What should I do if I need to cancel an appointment?

If you need to cancel an appointment:

  1. Go to the "Appointments" section in your client portal
  2. Find the appointment you wish to cancel
  3. Click on the "Cancel" button
  4. Provide a reason for cancellation (optional)
  5. Confirm the cancellation

Please note that we request at least 24 hours notice for cancellations. Late cancellations may incur a fee as per our cancellation policy.

How can I view my pet's medical records?

To view your pet's medical records:

  1. Go to the "My Pets" section in your client portal
  2. Select the pet whose records you want to view
  3. Click on the "Medical Records" tab

Here you'll find a complete history of your pet's visits, vaccinations, prescriptions, and treatment plans. You can also download or print these records if needed.
